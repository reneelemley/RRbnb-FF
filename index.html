The location

On the hilltop in Point Blanche, with St. Barths on the horizon.

Perched securely at the top of the hill on Sint Maarten's quiet southeastern tip — gated, private, and looking straight out toward St. Barths. Not steps from the sand, but the view more than makes up for it.

It's an easy 15-minute drive to the French side, where the restaurants and beaches are spectacular. Dutch-side beaches are closer, but timing around traffic helps.
